About Us

Quality Is Our Watch Word

With over two decades of experience in the heavy machine industry, we have transformed Craine Machines from a modest startup into one of Nigeria's premier production equipment providers. The journey began with a simple vision: to ensure only quality machines are imported into Nigeria.

"Our success is built on trust and relationships. Every machine we sell, every customer we serve, adds to our story of empowering Nigerian entrepreneurs. Our hands-on approach and deep understanding of both technical and business aspects have been instrumental in building Craine machines reputation for excellence.

Key Milestones

  • Founded Craine Machines in 2015
  • Established partnerships with leading global manufacturers
  • Expanded services nationwide
  • Built a team of certified technicians
  • Launched comprehensive after-sales support program

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Craine Machines began as a small family business with the knowledge of heavy production machines and how to import quality machines into Nigeria.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality Production equipment and machines that empower both small and large scale businesses.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's leading provider of heavy machines and equipment, customer service, and industry expertise.
